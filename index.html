Frequently Asked Questions – Bakery Services

Do you accept custom cake orders?+

Yes — we accept custom cake orders with at least 3 days notice. Contact us to discuss designs, flavors, and pricing for your celebration.

Are your breads made with sourdough?+

Many of our artisan loaves are naturally fermented using long fermentation techniques for better flavor, digestibility, and shelf life.

Do you offer catering services?+

Yes. We offer professional catering for events — reach out with your event date, guest count, and dietary requirements for a personalized quote.

Our Story – Local Artisan Bakery in Polokwane

Baked with Passion Since 2015

At Baker's Delights, we believe that great baking is an art form. Founded in 2015 in the heart of Polokwane, our artisan bakery has been serving the community with fresh, high-quality pastries, breads, and cakes daily.

We source only the finest local ingredients and follow time-honored recipes to ensure every bite is perfect. Whether it's a flaky croissant, custom celebration cake, or naturally fermented sourdough bread, we put our heart into every creation.
